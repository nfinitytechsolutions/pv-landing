From published case report to a reviewed, E2B-ready ICSR

1

Screen

Ingests literature and flags valid ICSRs vs noise using a transparent rule — not a black box.

2

Extract & code

Pulls patient, suspect drug, and adverse events; suggests MedDRA PT/SOC for the coder to confirm.

3

Draft

Generates a factual case narrative from the structured fields — the associate edits, never re-types.

4

Review & export

A qualified human approves; the case exports as ICH ICSR (E2B) XML for Argus, ARISg, or Veeva Vault Safety.

Why on-premise is the whole point

  • Zero third-party egress. The model, your data, and the audit trail stay inside your environment or VPC. Demonstrable with the network disconnected.
  • Validation-ready. Append-only audit trail, model/prompt version pinning, and explainable rules — designed for 21 CFR Part 11 / GAMP 5 / GVP.
  • Assistive, never autonomous. The AI drafts; a qualified human verifies and signs off every case. It does not replace medical review.
  • Fits your stack. Exports standard E2B — feeds your existing safety database instead of replacing it.
